WCU honors group raises money for hunger in Africa

April 13, 2014

Author: Kendal Gapinski, Daily Local News

The Honors Student Association at West Chester University announced that it had raised more than $11,000 during its ninth annual Aid to South Africa event.

The event featured musical performances, carnival games, raffle prizes, food, jousting, a dunk tank and many other activities, the association said.

“The event was spectacular,” Assistant Director and honors sophomore, Emily Roth added. “The weather turned out perfect, and we couldn’t ask for more hard working volunteers.”

The Honors Student Association is a campus organization that focuses on service and leadership development, according to the university. It has approximately 180 active members.
